# Official Web Resources on Oral Health: Patient Information Directories

This directory compiles high-quality, verified patient information resources on oral health maintained by leading global, national public health authorities and established clinical institutions.

---

## 🌎 Global & Institutional Authorities

### World Health Organization (WHO)
* **[WHO Oral Health Topic Page](https://www.who.int/health-topics/oral-health)**
  * **Target Audience:** General public, health educators, and global health advocates.
  * **Content Provided:** Global facts regarding oral disease, prevention strategies, interactive infographics highlighting the public burden of cavities, and policy briefs detailing the direct link between dietary sugar consumption and dental health.

### National Institute of Dental and Craniofacial Research (NIDCR)
* **[NIDCR Digital Publications & Health Info](https://www.nidcr.nih.gov/health-info/publications)**
  * **Target Audience:** Patients and researchers seeking comprehensive, science-backed material on specific medical or structural conditions.
  * **Content Provided:** Highly detailed, multi-language patient guides addressing topics such as the chemical process of cavity reversal, Chronic Dry Mouth (Xerostomia), Temporomandibular Disorders (TMD/TMJ), Oral Cancer, and step-by-step guidance on how to access low-cost dental services.

## 🇺🇸 United States Government Authorities

### Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
* **[CDC Division of Oral Health Portal](https://www.cdc.gov/oral-health/index.html)**
  * **Target Audience:** Adults, parents, community health organizers, and public health practitioners.
  * **Content Provided:**
    * *About Oral Health Basics:* Accessible, structured breakdowns detailing the stages of tooth decay and periodontal (gum) diseases.
    * *Oral Health and You:* Targeted physiological dental advice tailored independently for children, adults, and pregnant individuals.
    * *Community Resources:* Patient factsheets and public statements regarding the long-term benefits of community water fluoridation and school-based dental sealant programs.

## 🇬🇧 United Kingdom Public Health Authorities

### National Health Service (NHS)
* **[NHS Healthy Teeth and Gums Guide](https://www.nhs.uk/live-well/healthy-teeth-and-gums/take-care-of-your-teeth-and-gums/)**
  * **Target Audience:** General healthcare patients seeking evidence-based dental hygiene routines.
  * **Content Provided:** Practical checklists for daily dental care (proper brushing techniques and flossing intervals), systemic lifestyle advice relating to sugars, pediatric dental timeline guides, and plain-language breakdowns of common therapeutic dental interventions.

### GOV.UK (Department of Health and Social Care)
* **[Oral Health Assessment Patient Information Booklet](https://assets.publishing.service.gov.uk/media/5a7c4ee9e5274a2041cf3204/dh_132576.pdf)**
  * **Target Audience:** General active dental patients preparing for standard or preventative clinical check-ups.
  * **Content Provided:** A downloadable public guide clarifying what an official clinical oral health assessment covers, how a personalized dental care plan is formulated, and the medical necessity of transparently sharing general health backgrounds with a dental professional.

## 🩺 Leading Professional & Clinical Associations

### American Dental Association (ADA)
* **[MouthHealthy.org](https://www.mouthhealthy.org/)**
  * **Target Audience:** Families, expectant mothers, seniors, and individuals seeking customized life-stage dental care.
  * **Content Provided:** An extensive, easily navigable repository that features a "Find-a-Dentist" mapping tool, an catalog of verified commercial products bearing the official *ADA Seal of Acceptance*, and consumer-friendly patient education sheets dealing with symptoms, emergency responses, and cosmetic procedures.

### Oral Health Foundation
* **[Dental Health Library](https://www.dentalhealth.org/)**
  * **Target Audience:** Patients, schools, community educators, and corporate wellness officers.
  * **Content Provided:** A peer-reviewed, plain-English glossary of dental terminology, specialized advisory columns tailored for separate age brackets, and actionable steps to rapidly self-identify the early warning indicators of gum disease and oral ulcers.

### Mayo Clinic
* **[Oral Health: A Window to Your Overall Health](https://www.mayoclinic.org/healthy-lifestyle/adult-health/in-depth/dental/art-20047475)**
  * **Target Audience:** Medical patients, caregivers, and individuals tracking systemic health connections.
  * **Content Provided:** A clinically structured informational essay mapping out the bi-directional pathway between oral microbiomes and systemic medical conditions, highlighting clinical correlations with diabetes, cardiovascular illness, endocarditis, and pregnancy risks.

  ## 🇪🇺 European Authorities & Specialized Clinical Societies

### European Federation of Periodontology (EFP)
* **[EFP Patient Portal](https://www.efp.org/for-patients/home/)**
  * **Target Audience:** Individuals seeking to evaluate their gum health, dental implant patients, and expectant mothers.
  * **Content Provided:** * *Gum Health Self-Assessment:* An interactive tool for checking symptoms of gingivitis or periodontitis.
    * *Systemic Health Projects:* Evidence-based public campaigns tracing the connection of oral health to secondary issues (*Perio & Cardio*, *Perio & Diabetes*, and *Oral Health & Pregnancy*).

### The European Society of Endodontology (ESE)
* **[ESE Patient Resources](https://www.e-s-e.eu/for-patients/patient-resources/)**
  * **Target Audience:** Patients experiencing tooth pain or undergoing complex internal tooth treatments.
  * **Content Provided:** Easy-to-understand multi-lingual video animations detailing specialized therapies, vital pulp treatments, and consumer advice warning against the misuse of antibiotics for minor toothaches.

### Platform for Better Oral Health in Europe
* **[Oral Health Best Practices](https://www.oralhealthplatform.eu/our-best-practices/)**
  * **Target Audience:** General public, community educators, and European healthcare policy advocates.
  * **Content Provided:** Documentation showcasing effective preventative programs deployed across EU member states, emphasizing structural fluoridation, dietary modifications regarding sugar, and life-course approach guidelines.

## Catalan & Authorities

### Col·legi Oficial d'Odontòlegs i Estomatòlegs de Catalunya (COOEC)
* **[COOEC Portal](https://www.cooec.cat/)**
  * **Target Audience:** Patients, dental professionals, and residents of Catalonia seeking accredited local providers.
  * **Content Provided:** Official register of licensed dentists in Catalonia, patient rights and responsibilities under Catalan healthcare law, professional ethical guidelines, and public awareness campaigns on oral disease prevention tailored to the Catalan population.

### Consell de Dentistes de Catalunya
* **[Consell de Dentistes Web](https://www.conselldentistes.cat/)**
  * **Target Audience:** General public, patients, and institutional partners within Catalonia.
  * **Content Provided:** Institutional press releases on regional oral health policies, patient complaint and mediation services for dental treatments, and directories of associated dental professionals practicing in Catalonia.

### Agència de Salut Pública de Catalunya (ASPCAT / Canal Salut)
* **[Canal Salut – Salut Bucodental](https://canalsalut.gencat.cat/ca/salut-a-z/s/salut-bucodental/)**
  * **Target Audience:** Catalan residents, parents, schools, and primary care users.
  * **Content Provided:** Official Government of Catalonia guidelines on community water fluoridation, school-based oral health check-ups (Programa de Salut Bucodental Escolar), preventive advice for children and pregnant women, and links to the public dental health network (PADES / Xarxa de Salut Bucodental).

### Hospital Clínic de Barcelona – Servei d'Odontologia
* **[Hospital Clínic – Odontologia](https://www.clinicbarcelona.org/ca/assistencia/area-integral-de-salut/odontologia)**
  * **Target Audience:** Patients seeking hospital-based specialized dental care in Barcelona.
  * **Content Provided:** Information about public and private specialist consultations in oral surgery, periodontics, pediatric dentistry, and maxillofacial pathology, with referral procedures and pre-surgical guidelines.
